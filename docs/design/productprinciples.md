# Product Principles:
1. A Cairn is a meaningful place.

A Cairn is more than a coordinate on a map.

It represents experiences, memories, and stories.


2. Places come first.

Maps are how you navigate.

Places are why you return.

3. Privacy by default.

Every Cairn belongs to you.

Sharing is always intentional.

4. Build memories, not content.

No likes.

No followers.

No algorithms.

No feeds.

5. Simplicity over features.

If a feature doesn't help someone remember a place, it probably doesn't belong.

# A Cairn Is
A Cairn consists of:

• Location
• Name
• Photos
• Notes
• Tags
• Favorite
• Created Date
• Last Visited

# User Stories

I found an incredible dispersed campsite at sunset.

I want to save it in under 30 seconds.

Next year I want to find it again.

---

My daughter asks where we camped three years ago.

I open Cairn.

We look at the photos together.

--- 

My best friend is planning a trip.

I share three Cairns with him.

Nobody else can see them.

--- 

I had a childhood camping spot but I don't remember where it was. I asked my brother and he remembers where, I need to write it down somewhere.
