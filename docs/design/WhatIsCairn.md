# What is Cairn?

Cairn is a simple place journal for remembering meaningful outdoor locations.

Found a great car camping spot at the end of a forest service road? Build a Cairn for it.

Have years of saved Google Maps locations that are hard to organize or revisit? Bring them into Cairn so you can see your places in one map, remember when you last visited, and add notes, photos, and stories over time.

Cairn is not a social network. It is not a public database of hidden spots. It is a personal place journal first, a sharing tool second, and never an app built around likes, followers, or feeds.

A Cairn is a meaningful place.

A Cairn is more than a coordinate on a map. It is a place tied to experiences, memories, and stories. Cairn helps people discover, remember, and return to the places that matter.
